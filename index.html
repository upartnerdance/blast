All day, dancers perform in the format of a dance competition and receive feedback from experienced ballroom pros. There'll be fun dances, games, and giveaways throughout the day, as well as makeup and jewelry vendors to help participants look smashing.

In the evening session, enjoy games, social dancing, spotlight dances, and performances by national championship-level dancers.
