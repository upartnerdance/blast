Here's What to Expect:

All day, dancers perform in the format of a dance competition and receive feedback from experienced ballroom professionals. There'll be fun dances, games, and giveaways, as well as makeup and jewelry vendors to help participants look fantastic.

In the evening session, enjoy games, social dancing, spotlight dances, and performances by national championship-level dancers.
