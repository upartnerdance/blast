Pricing

Competitor package: $40 per person for up to 8 entries.

  • Competitor package: up to 8 amateur event entries for $40 per person. Includes a program and admission to the ballroom for both the day session and the evening Halloween Ball.
  • Your first mixed proficiency event is free--program and admission to the Halloween Ball in the evening not included.
  • More than one MP event = $40 competitor package (see above).
  • MP events are always free for the more proficient partner.

Limits

Here's how your entries are limited:

  • Each person may enter any event only once.
  • Each person may enter up to 8 amateur events.
  • You may only dance 1 mixed proficiency event with a given partner.
  • You may not dance mixed proficiency and amateur with the same partner.
